# Amazoid - Mysterious Mailbox Merchant

A Project Zomboid mod that adds a mysterious merchant service through mailboxes.

## Features

### Core Mechanics
- **Discovery**: Random letters appear in mailboxes offering a mysterious delivery service
- **Catalog System**: Order items by marking catalogs and leaving money in the mailbox
- **Reputation System**: Build trust through payments and missions to unlock more content
- **Crafted/Found Mailboxes**: Upgrade to larger mailboxes for bigger items

### Catalogs
- **Basic Catalog** (Rep 0+): Food, drinks, basic supplies
- **Tools Catalog** (Rep 10+): Hammers, saws, building materials
- **Weapons Catalog** (Rep 25+): Melee weapons, ammunition
- **Medical Catalog** (Rep 25+): Bandages, medicine, medical supplies
- **Seasonal Catalog** (Rep 35+): Items that change with the seasons
- **Black Market** (Rep 50+): Firearms, military gear, rare items

### Reputation
- **Increases by**: Overpaying, completing missions
- **Decreases by**: Underpaying, failing missions, keeping scavenger hunt items
- **Unlocks**: New catalogs, discounts, missions, and lore

### Mission Types
- **Collection**: Gather and deliver specific items
- **Elimination**: Kill zombies with specific weapons
- **Scavenger Hunt**: Find marked zombies carrying valuable items
- **Timed Delivery**: Rush packages to other locations
- **Protection**: Defend a noisy device from zombie attacks

### Merchant Gifts
The mysterious merchants observe your actions and occasionally send gifts:
- Using rags to heal → Bandages
- Surviving milestones → Supplies
- Kill streaks → Weapons

### Lore & Story
- Discover the identity of the merchants through reputation and exploration
- The merchants are revealed to be Marcus & Julia, a couple with 2 cats and a dog
- Find their hidden cabin in the woods for the ultimate meeting

## Installation

1. Place the `Amazoid` folder in your `Zomboid/Workshop/` directory
2. Enable the mod in the Project Zomboid mod manager
